The Discovery of Coffee

There is a legend that describes an Ethiopian monk who first discovered coffee. When herding animals, he noticed that they would become energetic when eating the berries containing coffee (yes, coffee is a berry. It's the bean inside the berry that we all know and love). This monk's name was Kaldi, who reported this finding to his abbot (the head monk). The abbot made a drink from these beans, and to his wonder, he found that it made him energetic and awake enough to endure the long hours of his prayers.

Soon, knowledge of this discovery began to spread. Farmers and merchants across the Arabian peninsula began growing and selling these magic beans as their popularity soard. A drink that can delay fatigue and tiredness? Who wouldn't want a taste of that!
